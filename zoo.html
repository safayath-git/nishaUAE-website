AL AIN ZOO


      Visit the great family-friendly attraction in the green city, Al Ain Zoo. It stands as a beautiful and safe home to more than 4000 animals of different kinds including Arabian oryx, rhinos, hippos, tigers, lions, and many more. The Sheikh Zayed Desert Learning Centre, housed in the zoo in a beautiful building boasting Leadership in Energy and Environmental Design (LEED) Platinum certification, is a fantastic place to learn more about the UAE's natural environment. Be sure to check out the zoo’s very recent addition, Al Ain Safari, which alone covers 215 plus hectares of land, remarkably representing one of the world’s most legendary ecosystems.

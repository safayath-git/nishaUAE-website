DUBAI DESERT SAFARI


Though mainly known for its great infrastructure and adventurous streaks, at the heart of Dubai is still its desolate desert. And so, the city presents various Desert Safari options to enjoy the desert a bit more effectively. The visitors can experience desert camping while enjoying Arabian cuisine, belly dancing, and other traditional performances, making it one of its kind and worth every penny spent! On the day safari, you can go on the adventurous rides of dune bashing, quad biking, sandboarding, camel rides, and more.
